How to Play

• You have 6 minutes to solve as many challenges as possible. Score increases by 1 for each correct answer.

• Easy: Map symbols from top to bottom row directly.

• Moderate: Top row → 1 permutation step → bottom row.

• Hard: Top row → 2 permutation steps → bottom row.

• Select the correct number sequence that maps current symbols to the final row.
